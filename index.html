About Me

I am a systems architect and developer obsessed with building the infrastructure for the next generation of AI. My work focuses on creating high-performance Go backends, defining open standards like the Universal Tool Calling Protocol (UTCP), and engineering autonomous agents that can reason and act.

From designing lock-free event buses to implementing protocol buffers for GraphQL, I specialize in solving complex architectural challenges. I am a core contributor to Protocol Lattice, where I help shape the tools and frameworks that allow AI models to interact seamlessly with the real world.
